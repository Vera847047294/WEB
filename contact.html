Shipping & Return Policies

Everything you need to know about ordering with us

Shipping Information

  • Free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the contiguous US
  • Standard shipping: $7.95 for orders under $50
  • Express shipping: $15.95 (2-3 business days)
  • Overnight shipping: $29.95 (next business day)
  • Terrariums are carefully packaged with protective materials to ensure safe arrival
  • Tracking information provided via email once order ships
  • We ship Monday through Wednesday to avoid weekend delays

Returns & Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your terrarium. If you're not happy with your purchase:

  • 30-day satisfaction guarantee: Return unused terrariums in original packaging for a full refund
  • Damaged items: Contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund, no questions asked
  • Custom orders: Due to their personalized nature, custom terrariums are final sale unless damaged
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days of receiving returned item

Product Warranty

Every Terra Microscape terrarium comes with our comprehensive warranty:

  • 90-day plant guarantee: If your plants don't thrive, contact us for replacement plants or store credit
  • Lifetime glass warranty: If your glass container cracks under normal use, we'll replace it at no charge
  • 30-day adjustment period: Complimentary adjustments if your terrarium needs minor corrections
  • Warranty does not cover damage from improper care, neglect, or unauthorized modifications

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Ordering

How long does it take to process an order?

Standard orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Custom orders take 2-3 weeks for design and production.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Standard orders can be modified within 24 hours of placement. Contact us immediately for any changes.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! We offer premium gift wrapping for $5. Add a handwritten note for an extra $3.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Shipping

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the continental US only. International shipping coming soon!

What if my terrarium arrives damaged?

Document any damage with photos within 24 hours of delivery. We'll send a replacement immediately.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Tracking information is emailed once your order ships. You can also create an account to view order status.

Do you offer local pickup?

We don't offer physical storefront pickup, but local delivery may be available in the Portland area.

Care

How often should I water my terrarium?

Most terrariums need water every 3-6 months. Forest types every 4-6 months, rainforest every 3-4 months, desert every 6-12 months.

What light do terrariums need?

Most do well in bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sun which can overheat the closed environment.

What if my plants look unhealthy?

Contact us with photos! Our 90-day plant guarantee covers unhealthy plants. We'll advise on care adjustments or send replacements.

Can I add more plants later?

We don't recommend adding plants to established terrariums. Instead, consider starting a new one or our custom design service.

Custom Orders

How does the custom design process work?

Submit an inquiry form with your vision. We'll schedule a consultation, create design drafts, then craft your terrarium.

How much do custom terrariums cost?

Custom terrariums start at $150 and go up based on complexity, size, and plant selection. Corporate bulk orders have special pricing.

Can you match my brand colors?

Absolutely! We specialize in brand-matched designs for corporate gifts and events.

What's the turnaround for custom orders?

Typical turnaround is 2-3 weeks from consultation to delivery. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

Can't Find What You're Looking For?

Our team is here to help with any questions about terrariums, orders, or custom designs.
